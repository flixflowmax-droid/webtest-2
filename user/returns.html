Return Policy

  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receiving your order.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have original tags attached.
  • Final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged.
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

How to Return

To initiate a return, log into your Member Dashboard, select the order you wish to return, and follow the instructions to print a return shipping label.
